About me

Hello, I'm Samuel! I'm a Software Engineering student from Sweden. Welcome to my little personal page! On here you'll find my personal projects, and my blog, a place for me to write down my thoughts as well as what's going on my life. This is mainly for me to look back at in a few years to see how I have developed as a developer, but feel free to read if anything seems interesting!

As a programmer, I am interested in learning fullstack, and my planned projects are in this area. My two favourite things are designing stuff and keeping my code clean. Agile methods is also something I find very interesting, and is something I will write about as well.

Check out my GitHub or LinkedIn!

Coding

I tend to lean towards high-level languages, and I think this is clearly reflected in my used languages.

  • Python: my QnD language of choice, I love it (numpy has been a great replacement for MATLAB in school)
  • Java: my strongest language, somehow... I have learned a lot of OOP centered around it in school, and it is kind of fun to use (as long as a GUI isn't involved)
  • TypeScript: a very pleasant language to use for web development, and I find it very nice to work with when using React
  • Go: I have used it for work, and I like the syntax a lot, I am definitely looking forward to learning more about it
